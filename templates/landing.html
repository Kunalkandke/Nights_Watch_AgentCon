What It Does
End-to-End Compliance Automation
From raw PDF to board-ready audit report — fully automated, no manual steps.
Agent 1

Smart Document Intake

Extracts patient name, doctor, diagnosis, treatment, consent status, signatures, and dates from any medical PDF.

Agent 2

RAG Compliance Check

Cross-references against hospital SOPs, AIIMS guidelines, MLC protocols, and discharge formats using Retrieval-Augmented Generation.

Agent 3

Risk Assessment

Classifies documents as High / Medium / Low risk. Identifies patient safety gaps, legal liability exposure, and audit failure probability.

Agent 4

Audit Report Generator

Produces executive summary, medical chronology, compliance verdict (PASS / FAIL), and prioritized action items for hospital management.

Live Compliance Dashboard

Track all analyzed documents, compliance scores, risk levels, and verdicts in a clean, sortable dashboard built for audit teams.

LangGraph Orchestration

All 4 agents are wired into a LangGraph StateGraph — deterministic, traceable, and production-ready workflow execution.
